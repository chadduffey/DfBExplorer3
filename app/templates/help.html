New Accounts
To create a new account:

  1. Navigate to www.dbexplorer.herokuapp.com

  2. Select “Log In”
  3. Select “New User”
  4. Enter your details
  5. Click register
  6. You will be emailed a confirmation to the email address you register with.
  7. Click on the link to verify the account in the confirmation email.
  8. You will be directed back to the login page.
  9. Log in with your new account.
